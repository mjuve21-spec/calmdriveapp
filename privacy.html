Privacy Policy for CalmDrive

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Introduction

CalmDrive ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the CalmDrive mobile application.

Information We Collect

CalmDrive does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data stays on your device.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Your name (if you choose to provide it)
  • Number of drives logged
  • App preferences

This information is stored only on your device using browser local storage and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any other personal data

Third-Party Services

CalmDrive does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

CalmDrive does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. You can clear all app data at any time through your browser settings.

Offline Functionality

CalmDrive works completely offline after initial installation. No internet connection is required or used during normal operation.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data by clearing browser storage or uninstalling the app
  • Use the app without providing any personal information
  • Request information about what data is stored (which is only the items listed above)

Data Deletion

To delete all your CalmDrive data:

  1. Clear your browser's local storage for the CalmDrive website, or
  2. Uninstall the CalmDrive app from your device

This will permanently remove all locally stored data.
